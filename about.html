Our Story

Altiva was born from a personal journey. It all started with my dad, who loves to trade but was struggling to keep track of his investments. Watching him juggle multiple notebooks and spreadsheets inspired me to create a better solution.

As a solo developer passionate about both technology and finance, I combined these interests to build Altiva - an intuitive investment tracking app that simplifies portfolio management for everyone, from seasoned traders to beginners.

Today, Altiva helps thousands of users track their investments, analyze performance, and make informed decisions - all from their mobile devices.
